# Privacy Policy

**Calendar Heatmap for Power BI**
Publisher: Datayaga

## Data Collection

Calendar Heatmap for Power BI does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All data processing happens entirely within the Power BI environment on your device or your organization's Power BI service.

## What the Visual Accesses

The visual only accesses the data fields you explicitly assign to it in the Power BI field wells (Date, Value, Tooltip Fields, Annotation). This data never leaves Power BI.

## Third-Party Services

This visual does not connect to any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. It has no network dependencies.

## Cookies and Tracking

This visual does not use cookies, analytics, telemetry, or any form of user tracking.

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with the updated date.
